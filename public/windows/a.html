Profile

Energy efficient window, made of extruded, impact-resistant and climate-resistant PVC profiles with very good insulating properties, which provide good U-value. The U-value for the window is determined by the insulating ability of the glass. Enova recommends windows with 3-layer energy-saving glass. Frame depth 90 mm. PVC profiles are completely recyclable.

  • + Designed for harsh Nordic climate
  • + Great, classic design
  • + Good U-value
  • + Maintenance-friendly
  • + Long service life
  • + Recyclable

Reinforcement

Hot-dip galvanized steel reinforcements. The reinforcements are installed into profiles for extra strength and stability.

Handle

Hoppe aluminium handle, the standard is aluminium-colored but can also be obtained in black and white.

Fittings/Accessories

High quality aluminium hinges from Spilka with stainless steel pin A4.

Automatic Safety and Air Barrier

The combined air ventilation and safety locking function launches automatically when the window is opened.

Air Ventilation

All our windows can be supplied with a Spilvent Click-vent in the upper frame. The Click-vent is very tight in the closed position, and in the open position it leads the air into the room.
